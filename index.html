Welcome to TinyFX
Hope you're enjoying TinyFX so far! If you like what I've built, feel free to share it with other creators. Every sound you make helps keep this project alive!
Overview
* Create: A simple tool for making your own sound effects. Just move the sliders and listen to the sound change!
* Seeds: You can copy a seed and paste it into any other TinyFX instance to load & edit that effect there!
* Management: Double-click saved items to rename or delete.
For your information..
Feel free to use any sounds you make here however you like. games, music, or videos. No credit needed for a second long audio effect, but sharing the tool with others is always appreciated!
